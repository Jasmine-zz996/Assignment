The Boy and the Heron

《The Boy and the Heron》is an animated film directed and written by Hayao Miyazaki, produced by Studio Ghibli. It was released in Japan on July 14, 2023, in Taiwan, China on October 6 of the same year, in Hong Kong, China on December 14, and in Chinese mainland on April 3, 2024.

The film tells the story of the young boy Mahito Maki, whose mother dies in a fire. He moves in with his father and stepmother to form a new family, but remains consumed by grief, withdrawn and unable to adapt to his new life. One day, by accident, he follows a talking grey heron into an abandoned, mysterious tower and stumbles into a fantastical “world of the dead”, beginning an incredible adventure.
