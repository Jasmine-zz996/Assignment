Character Introductions

Mahito Maki

Voiced by: Soma Santoki (Japanese), Tseng Ching-hua (Taiwan, China), Liu Haoran (Chinese mainland), Luca Padovan (English)
The protagonist. A boy consumed by loss, he loses his mother in the Pacific War air raids and moves with his father to live with his stepmother. Drawn into another world, he matures through extraordinary experiences.
The Grey Heron

Voiced by: Masaki Suda (Japanese), Greg Hsu (Taiwan, China), Da Peng (Chinese mainland), Robert Pattinson (English)
A heron who lures Mahito into the other world. He is actually the “Heron Man” who can take human form. He acts as Mahito's guide in the other world, with Mahito holding onto his precious wing.
Himi

Voiced by: Aimyon (Japanese), Chiang Ching-yen (Taiwan, China), Zhang Hanyun (Chinese mainland), Karen Fukuhara (English)
A young girl Mahito meets in the other world. The young Kiriko in the other world addresses her as “Lady Himi”. She can emerge from flames and use fire to drive away pelicans.
Shoichi Maki

Voiced by: Takuya Kimura (Japanese), Hsieh Yi-fan (Taiwan, China), Zhu Yawen (Chinese mainland), Christian Bale (English)
Mahito's father, an industrialist who runs an aircraft factory. After his wife Hisako dies, he marries her younger sister Natsuko. A capable businessman, he often acts impulsively without considering Mahito's feelings.
Natsuko Maki

Voiced by: Yoshino Kimura (Japanese), Angelica Lee (Taiwan, China), Gao Yuanyuan (Chinese mainland), Maureen Chen (English)
Mahito's stepmother and the biological younger sister of Mahito's birth mother Hisako. Natsuko and Hisako both grew up in a house with a mysterious tower. After becoming pregnant, troubled by her complicated relationship with Mahito, she enters the other world on her own.
Kiriko

Voiced by: Kou Shibasaki (Japanese), Chien Hsin-yu (Taiwan, China), Florence Pugh (English)
A female crew member Mahito first meets in the other world. She is the form taken by the elderly maid Kiriko from the mansion, who enters the other world alongside Mahito and watches over him in this guise.
Old Pelican

Voiced by: Kaoru Kobayashi (Japanese), Chen Kuo-wei (Taiwan, China), Willem Dafoe (English)
Pelicans inhabit the other world and prey on the Warawara, souls about to be reborn as humans. An elderly pelican meets Mahito near the end of his life and tells him of the pelicans' fate in this world.
Great Uncle

Voiced by: hohei Hino (Japanese), Sun Chung-tai (Taiwan, China), Chen Jianbin (Chinese mainland), Mark Hamill (English)
The “Master” who invites Mahito to the other world, hoping he will become his heir. He is the great uncle of Hisako and Natsuko, the one who built the other world by stacking stone piles.
Parakeet King

Voiced by: Jun Kunimura (Japanese), Wang Sui-chiang (Taiwan, China), Zhu Yawen (Chinese mainland), Dave Bautista (English)
Leads a large army of parakeets in the other world. He respects the Great Uncle, the creator of the other world, yet also plans to trade Himi to him.
